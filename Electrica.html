Rules

Total 3 rounds

RULES FOR ROUND 1 :

1.This round consist of technical quiz on fundamentals of Electronics and Electrical engineering.
2. Participants need to solve 25 MCQ , the time of submission and score will be judging criterion .
3. Maximum time for the first round is 20 minutes.
4. Use of scientific calculator is allowed.
5. Participation should be in groups containing minimum 2 or maximum 3 students .

Instructions Round 2 :

1. In this round you will be given three questions that are to be solved and write answers for all on a paper which you will need to submit in or before time.
Question 1: Word search
Question 2 : Puzzle Question
Question 3 : Jumbled words

2. Remember to submit and put your group name on the answer sheet.
3. Maximum time for Round 3 is 30 minutes.
4. Submission time will be considered for declaration of round 3
5. Try to get as many words as you can.

Instructions Round 3 :

1. This is final round of electrica event.
At the end of this round, three groups will be declared as winners .
2. Students will play technical Snakes and Ladder and try to reach to destination by decoding the clues.
3. The dice will be played to reach to the next place.
4. Out of 3 players one student will be playing Snakes and Ladders and two students will help the team member to reach the top or destination by solving the hurdles.
5. Time and total score from all three rounds will be considered for declaration of winners.
6..Maximum time for round is 40 minutes.
