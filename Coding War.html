Rules


1. Individual students in First year around Maharashtra state are allowed to compete.

2. The decision of judges and coordinating committee will be final and binding on all participants.

3. All digital gadgets such as watches , smartphones, headphones (except laptop) are strictly prohibited. If found will be disqualified.

4. Results of all Rounds will be decided by total number of problems solved correctly in least amount of time.

5. All details regarding rules , modifications , etc will be provided to participants.

Note:-

1. If possible bring your own laptop just in case or else we are providing you with a desktop.

2. These rules are tentative rules and may subject to minor changes.

Event Format


1. The event will consist of three rounds.

2. Round 1 :- Offline coding contest of 1 hours which will consist of 7 questions.

3. Round 2 :- Offline Logical questions MCQ test of 1 hour of 12 questions.

4. Round 3 :- Offline Coding contest of 1 hours of 4 questions.

5. Coding Platform : Hackerank.

Note:- Round 1, Round 2 and Round 3 will be physical mode (in college campus).
