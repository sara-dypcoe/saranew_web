Event

Robo Soccer, is a thrilling sport that combines robotics and artificial intelligence. In robot soccer, two teams of robots compete against each other on a specially designed field, trying to score more goals than their opponents. The objective of the game is to score goals by moving the ball into the opposing team's goal. The robots can kick the ball, and dribble it around the field. Whether you are a fan of sports or technology, robot soccer is definitely worth watching and exploring.

Rules

1. Each team can have upto 5 members max.

2. Each team can have only one robot for the full tournament. Exchange of robots is not allowed.

3. Only one player can control the robot for the whole match.

4. Player are not allowed to touch the robots in between the match.

Guidelines:

1. Power supply will not be provided on the board. The voltage of battery should not exceed 12V.

2. The dimensions of the must be below 40×40×40(in centimeters). The weight must be below 3.5kgs.

3. Organizer's decision will be final. And rules and regulations may change, without any prior notice.

4. The event will be conducted in two rounds.

Event Format

Round 1:-

It will consists 5 penalty shots. There will be obstacles in the field. If the robot or ball hit the obstacles, points will be deducted from the total.

Round 2:-

It will be knockout matches. The team which will score maximum goals within time will win the match.
