Event Format

Solving Mathamatical and Logical Puzzels

Registration: Students must pre-register for the event in advance to secure their spot.

Event format: The event will consist of multiple-choice questions and brain teasers, with a time limit for each question.

Scoring: Each correct answer will be awarded one point. There will be no penalty for incorrect answers.

Cheating: Cheating in any form will result in disqualification.

Conduct: Participants are expected to conduct themselves in a respectful and professional manner.

Tiebreaker: In case of a tie, a sudden-death tiebreaker round will be conducted.

Decisions: The decision of the event organizers will be final and binding.
