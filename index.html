    What is SARA!!!

    "Welcome to Spectrum, the ultimate celebration of technology and innovation! Spectrum is an annual event that brings together some of the brightest minds from across institutes to showcase their skills and ideas in the fields of science and technology. With a wide range of competitions, exhibitions, workshops, and talks, Spectrum offers a platform for participants to explore the latest advancements in technology and network with like-minded individuals.Participate as a college nominated team , represent your college and be part of esteemed Intercollegiate Championship Trophy. Our mission is to inspire and educate the next generation of tech leaders, and we do so by providing an immersive and engaging experience that combines education, entertainment, and innovation.
