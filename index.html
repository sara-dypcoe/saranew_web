    What is SARA!!!

    SARA - The Students Association of Robotics and Automation, is a student-led organization dedicated to advancing knowledge and innovation in the fields of robotics and automation. It provides a platform for students to engage in hands-on projects, collaborate on research, and participate in competitions. Through workshops, seminars, and networking events, SARA aims to foster a community of future engineers and technologists, helping them develop practical skills and stay at the forefront of technological advancements.
