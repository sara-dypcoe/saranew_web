Event

Tech Treasure Hunt is a competitive game that combines technology and problem-solving skills. Participants work in groups to solve clues and puzzles that lead them on a scavenger hunt around campus. The clues are intended to assess participants' understanding of technology and engineering concepts, as well as their ability to work as a team.


Participants in this competition must think creatively and critically as they navigate clues and overcome challenges. It's a fantastic opportunity for students to hone their problem-solving, communication, and teamwork skills. The competition is intended to be engaging and exciting, and it is an excellent way to foster student interest in technology and engineering.

Rules

1] Participants must form teams of a certain size and stick together for the duration of the competition.
2] The use of mobile phones or other electronic devices is strictly prohibited during the competition.
3] Participants must follow all safety guidelines and stay within the competition area's designated boundaries.
4] Before moving on to the next clue or puzzle, teams must solve each one in the correct order.
5] The team that arrives at the final destination with all clues solved in the shortest amount of time is declared the winner.
6] Any misbehavior or violation of the rules may result in disqualification.
7] Max 5 members in a team are allowed.
7] Only FY students can participate.

Event Format

Round 1 - Quiz Round

1. Only 1 Representative From Each Team Is Allowed To Participate
2.15 Questions Will Be There , +1 for correct response and -1 for incorrect response
3.Only 15 minutes are there for this round
4. Questions Will Be Based On General Physics

Round 2 - Buzzer Round

1. Only 2 Representatives From Each Team will be allowed to participate
2. Questions Will Be Displayed On Screen , There Will Be Same Marking Scheme as per quiz round
3. Buzzer Round Will Be Played Between 4 Teams At A Time
4. 5 Questions Will Be There in this round

Round 3- Treasure Hunt

1. Treasure Is Hidden in PCCOE campus
2. There Will Be 5 Clues For Each Team
3. Fifth Clue Will Be Common For Each Team
4. First Team Finds The Treasure Will Be Declared As a winner
5. 1 volunteer will be there with each team
